ABOUT ME

I love art

Angelica Rodriguez is currently attending The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley in the Masters program for Studio 2D. She is also planning on acquiring her certificate in Mexican American Studies. She graduated from the University of Texas Pan-American with her BFA in Art in May 2015. She plans to someday teach painting aw well as Mexican American history at a University level and continue her passion in painting.
